Explore New York like it's yours

A New York City guide sharing authentic places and experiences for travelers that want to live like a local, because who doesn’t?

explore the Neighborhood

Bushwick

Industrial warehouses covered in graffiti, any type of cuisine your heart desires and people covered in glitter eating tacos at 4 in the morning. It’s amazing.

Williamsburg

The place to look like a fitness model while walking your dog, shop at trendy thrift stores, and enjoy nightlife as you take in beautiful sunset views of the Manhattan Skyline.

Park Slope

Where people go when they want to start a family but still be hip. Here you’ll find tree-lined streets with farm-to-table restaurants all next to Prospect Park.

Eat, See and Do

Frenchy's

Straight From les rues of Paris to Brooklyn, Frenchy’s Food truck is a neighborhood staple. There's no turning down David’s beignets, quiches, croissants — everything is baked to absolute perfection!

Brooklyn Botanic Garden

An urban botanic haven with a Japanese garden, Bonsai Museum and Rose Garden. It’s free on Saturday mornings before 10am! Tags: Do, Crown Heights

House of Yes

The Brooklyn club named the 2nd best thing to do in the world, and a temple of expression dedicated to connection, creativity and celebrating life. Get your glitter ready!

Smorgasburg

This weekly food festival overlooks the East River and offers almost 100 vendors. Whatever you’re craving, you’ll find it here!
